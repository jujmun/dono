### 1. About these Terms

1.1 These Society Campaign Terms apply to Campaigns run on behalf of a Society ("**Society Campaigns**"), to the Society concerned, to each Responsible Individual, and to any student whose Connected Account receives Donations for a Society Campaign.

1.2 These Terms supplement the Dono Terms of Service and the Student Campaign Terms, both of which continue to apply to the individual students involved. Where these Terms conflict with either document, these Terms take precedence for Society Campaigns. Defined terms have the meanings given in the Terms of Service.

1.3 A Society is typically an unincorporated association and is not necessarily a separate legal person. Nothing in these Terms should be read as stating that a Society is always a separate legal person. **[COUNSEL REVIEW REQUIRED — allocation of obligations within unincorporated associations]**

1.4 Society approval, and the appearance of a Society or institution name on a Campaign, does not mean that the university, college or any institution operates, sponsors or endorses the Campaign or Dono.

### 2. Creating a Society Campaign

2.1 A student may fundraise on behalf of a Society. A student may not fundraise on behalf of another individual.

2.2 A Society Campaign must be approved through the relevant society page before it is presented as a Society Campaign.

2.3 The Society must register at least one named **Responsible Individual**, who must confirm that they have authority to act for the Society. Whether approval by two society officers is required is **[TO BE CONFIRMED BEFORE LAUNCH]**; the present minimum is one Responsible Individual.

2.4 The label "**Society approved**" means only that the society page approved the Campaign for presentation as a society campaign. It does not mean the institution endorses the Campaign, and it is not a Dono verification of any campaign statement.

### 3. Payment recipient and Merchant of Record

3.1 Donations to a Society Campaign are paid, in order of preference:

(a) to a Connected Account held for the Society, paying out to a society bank account; or
(b) where no suitable society account exists, to the named student's Connected Account and bank account.

3.2 In each case, the Connected Account holder is the Merchant of Record. The ability of each unincorporated Society to open the relevant Stripe account must be confirmed in practice. **[SUBJECT TO STRIPE CONFIGURATION]**

3.3 Where an individual student's Connected Account is used:

(a) that individual remains responsible to the Payment Provider and to Dono, including for refunds, chargebacks and negative balances;
(b) society approval does not transfer that responsibility;
(c) the campaign page must disclose who the legal recipient of the funds is; and
(d) the funded property still belongs to the Society under clause 5.

### 4. Responsibilities of the Society and Responsible Individual

4.1 The Society and the Responsible Individual must ensure that the Campaign complies with the Terms of Service, the Student Campaign Terms and these Terms, including the rules on permitted use of funds, reallocation, Material Changes, Surplus Funds, evidence and updates.

4.2 The Responsible Individual must: maintain evidence and receipts; publish required updates; notify Material Changes before implementation; respond to Dono requests within five working days **[TO BE CONFIRMED]**; and cooperate with refunds and investigations.

4.3 The Connected Account holder is responsible for refunds and chargebacks in respect of the Campaign, and authorises Dono to initiate an approved refund as a last resort in accordance with the Refund and Dispute Policy. **[SUBJECT TO STRIPE CONFIGURATION AND COUNSEL REVIEW]**

### 5. Ownership of funded property

5.1 Items or outputs funded by a Society Campaign belong to the Society, not to any individual officer or member, and must be used for the stated campaign purpose.

5.2 An outgoing officer must not retain funded property personally. If funded property is sold, the proceeds belong to the Society or must remain dedicated to society purposes.

5.3 The resale rules in the Student Campaign Terms apply: no acquisition with the intention of immediate resale or personal profit; no sale within 12 months without Dono's prior approval except for documented reasons (defective, unsuitable, replaced or no longer reasonably needed); proceeds of any pre-completion sale must be reapplied to the campaign purpose or refunded; disposal after genuine use for the campaign purpose is permitted.

### 6. Succession and transfer

6.1 Before the current Responsible Individual leaves the institution or the role, the Society must appoint a replacement Responsible Individual, who must expressly accept the outstanding duties relating to the Campaign, including evidence, update, refund and cooperation obligations.

6.2 Dono may refuse a proposed transfer. Where the Campaign is fully complete and all obligations are satisfied, Dono may decide that no transfer is needed.

6.3 Transfer of the Responsible Individual role, of the Society, or of the Connected Account recipient is a Material Change and must follow the Material Change process in the Student Campaign Terms.

6.4 Departure from the institution does not extinguish the departing individual's accrued obligations, including any personal responsibility they hold as Connected Account holder.

### 7. Refunds and failed recovery

7.1 Donors' refund rights and time limits are set out in the Donor Terms and the Refund and Dispute Policy.

7.2 If an approved refund cannot be recovered from the responsible Connected Account, the account holder remains contractually liable. Dono never pays refunds from its own funds and does not guarantee recovery. Dono may suspend or ban users, remove Campaigns, refer deliberate misuse to the institution, report suspected fraud to the police or another authority, and share relevant identity information with the Donor where lawful and reasonably necessary.

### 8. Enforcement

8.1 Dono may warn, restrict, suspend, remove or ban the Campaign, the Society's presence on the Platform, the Responsible Individual or the Connected Account holder in accordance with Part 10 of the Terms of Service. Dono cannot necessarily pause a payout already sent or recover money already withdrawn.

### 9. General
